Sloppy Joes

Description

A sandwich great for lunch with a filling of ground beef that has been seasoned with a sauce of tomatoes and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • ¾ cup ketchup
  • 3 teaspoons brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and green pepper.
  2. Drain off liquids.
  3. Stir in the garlic powder, mustard, ketchup, and brown sugar; mix thoroughly
  4. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
